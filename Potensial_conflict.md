## Tension around the world

Between the Khennuirian Empire and the Levalon Dynasty, a silent war of innovation is constantly waged. Khennuirian society is built on military-industrial dominance, leading the world in the development of heavy weaponry and armor. Conversely, the Levalon Dynasty remains the premier producer of civilian magical tech and the primary supplier for the Assembly of High Thrones (AHT). Tensions arise because Khennuirian corporations often attempt to "poach" Elven and Human mages from Levalon, promising them higher salaries and more resources for experimentation. This "brain drain" has fueled the rise of the right-wing Elven party in Levalon, who argue that their magical secrets are being stolen to fuel the war machines of the North.
The Volcanic Brinkmanship

---

In the southeast, the Zozzalyra Kingdom exists in a state of perpetual anxiety regarding Volcan Dhur. Because the Fire Dwarves and the Ironforge Clan control the forges and the cooling wards of the volcano, they effectively hold the Zozzalyra government hostage. If the Dwarves were to stop maintaining the elemental wards, the desert pyramids would become uninhabitable due to the rising heat. This has led the Zozzalyra Kingdom to seek secret alliances with the Ministry of Souls, hoping that necromantic labor could replace their dependence on Dwarven elementalism. This potential alliance with the undead is viewed with extreme suspicion by the Xeolia Empire, which sees any expansion of necromancy as a direct threat to the "superior races."


---

In the West, The Great Maelstrom serves as a natural barrier between Khennuirian territory and the central mainland, but it also represents a massive untapped resource of aquatic mana. The Khennuirian Empire has recently begun experimenting with deep-sea "siphons" to harvest this energy, a move that the Assembly of High Thrones warns could destabilize the ocean currents. The Levalon Dynasty fears that if the Maelstrom is disturbed, it could flood the swampy regions of Osh and Dheros, potentially unleashing the ancient horrors trapped within the ruins and disrupting the fragile balance of the western coastline.
